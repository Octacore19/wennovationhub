INCUBATION

The Wennovation Hub incubation program is to guide early stage entrepreneurs to test and validate their ideas using basic business tools as well as gather their first set of customers and pivot where necessary.

If you did not meet our last incubation cohort or your startup is not addressing the focus sector, we have you covered. For a paltry cost, you can enjoy our business incubation program with the benefit of dedicated workspace and one-on-one mentoring during your program.
